About-Us

Welcome to Kurma IT Services, we are a small startup ERP consulting firm where the energy meets innovation—with a dash of fun! We’re here to help businesses streamline operations, unlock their potential, and even enjoy the process while doing it. Who says ERP has to be boring? We are a team of entrepreneurs, tech enthusiasts, and ERP experts passionate about making enterprise solutions both accessible and impactful. As a developing business ourselves, we know the ups and downs of growing a business. That’s why we’re dedicated to providing practical, scalable solutions—delivered with a friendly and human touch.


Our Mission

To transform how businesses approach ERP by making it approachable, efficient, and even enjoyable. We believe great systems should empower teams, not overwhelm them, and we’re committed to creating solutions that drive real impact and make implementation jouney a little fun.

Our Vision

To be the go-to partner for organizations seeking to unlock their full potential through innovative ERP systems, delivering measurable results and lasting value.
