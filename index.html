About-Us

Kurma IT Services' mission and passion.


Kurma IT Services, we are passionate about helping businesses achieve their full potential through the implementation and optimization of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions. With a track record of success and a team of dedicated experts, we have been elevating efficiency and empowering growth for organizations of all sizes and industries. As a leading provider of ERP consulting services, we specialize in the seamless integration of Infor ERP solutions. Our expertise lies in implementing and optimizing Infor LN, Infor CloudSuite, and other cutting-edge Infor software solutions. Through our extensive experience and in-depth knowledge of Infor ERP, we enable businesses to unlock their true potential and gain a competitive edge in today's rapidly evolving marketplace.


Our Mission

To empower businesses by providing cutting-edge ERP solutions that drive operational efficiency, streamline processes, and foster sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To be the go-to partner for organizations seeking to unlock their full potential through innovative ERP systems, delivering measurable results and lasting value.
